Banking & Finance

Automating KYC & Risk Assessment Pipelines.

0% Time Reduction
$0 Annual Savings

The Challenge

A leading regional wealth management firm was struggling with an exponentially growing backlog of Know Your Customer (KYC) documentation. Analysts were spending 70% of their time manually extracting entity data from unstructured PDFs, emails, and physical scans.

This manual extraction not only bottlenecked client onboarding times to roughly 14 days, but human error rates during data entry occasionally triggered severe compliance flags, risking multi-million dollar fines under strict regional MAS guidelines.

The Solution

Coaxis engineered a completely autonomous, secure document-processing pipeline utilizing off-the-shelf OCR combined with a fine-tuned, privately deployed LLM. Using our Data Readiness methodology, we normalized incoming documentation immediately.

We then orchestrated a swarm of narrow AI agents: one to strictly extract structural data, one to verify data against external global sanction lists via secured APIs, and a final "Supervisor Agent" to compile the risk dossier. The human element was shifted entirely from "data entry" to final "decision review".

The Result

The onboarding pipeline was compressed from 14 days to under 4 hours. By removing manual data scraping, human error in the extraction process dropped to absolute zero. The firm repurposed its analytical talent toward actual wealth generation strategies instead of administrative overhead.

Supply Chain & Logistics

Dynamically Routing Global Freight With Autonomous AI.

0 Uptime Orchestration
0% SLA Violations Drop

The Challenge

A global logistics proxy faced daily disruptions due to geopolitical unpredictability and supply chain blockages. Operations managers were forced to monitor disparate dashboards across sea freight, air cargo, and land transport systems manually.

When an anomaly occurred (like a port strike or sudden weather event), the lag time to manually reroute freight resulted in significant penalty fees and degraded client trust.

The Solution

Coaxis implemented a continuous monitoring automation layer powered by advanced decisioning models. We integrated data feeds from global shipping APIs, weather stations, and the client's internal legacy ERP system using secure, robust RPA connectors.

Instead of merely alerting humans, the AI Agent was empowered with "Next-Best-Action" capabilities. It autonomously drafted rerouting plans, calculated the cost delta, and preemptively generated the necessary customs adjustment documentation, presenting the fully formed solution to the logistics manager for a single-click approval.

The Result

SLA violations plummeted by 45% within the first operational quarter. The system now operates 24/7 without needing a human to catch the initial warning signs, vastly improving supply chain resilience and protecting the firm's bottom line.
