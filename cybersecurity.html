How Our Cybersecurity AI Works

Three layers of AI-powered security that work together — from detection to prediction to response.

01

Ingest & Unify

All log sources — firewalls, EDR, SIEM, identity providers, cloud platforms — are streamed into a unified intelligence layer. No data silos. Complete visibility across your entire security perimeter in real time.

02

Detect & Score

AI models trained on millions of historical incidents analyse live data streams to identify threats and assign risk scores to every network zone. Pattern libraries grow with each new incident — improving accuracy over time.

03

When risk spikes or a genuine threat is identified, your team receives a plain-language alert with full context — what happened, why it matters, and what to do. No technical jargon. Actionable intelligence for every stakeholder.
