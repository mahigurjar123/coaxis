The Coaxis Thesis

Agency is the new UI.

We believe that in the coming decade, the traditional interface (buttons, menus, forms) will give way to the Agentic Interface. Systems will no longer wait for a human to tell them *how* to do a task; they will understand the *intent* and execute the objective autonomously.

Our research is focused on minimizing the 'Intent Gap'—the distance between a business strategy and its technological execution. By building agents that understand context, policy, and goals, we are erasing this gap forever.
