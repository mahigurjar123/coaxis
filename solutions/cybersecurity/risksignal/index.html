Live Traffic, Behaviour & Access Monitoring

RiskSignal AI watches every packet, every login, every device interaction across your network in real time. It builds dynamic behavioural baselines for each zone — so when something deviates, it knows immediately and adjusts the risk score accordingly.

Real-time traffic analysis across all network segments and endpoints simultaneously
Device and user behaviour profiling — detects anomalous access patterns before abuse escalates

Under the Hood

How RiskSignal AI Works

A continuously running predictive intelligence engine that turns network behaviour data into actionable risk scores — in real time.

01

Historical Incident Learning

Pre-trained on years of security incident data and then fine-tuned on your organisation's own historical logs. It builds a precise model of what "risky" looks like in your specific environment, not just generic threat signatures.

Transfer LearningDomain Fine-TuningIncident Corpus
02

Real-Time Behavioural Baseline

Continuous monitoring of traffic patterns, authentication events, device behaviour, and access patterns establishes dynamic baselines for each network zone. Normal changes over time — RiskSignal adapts automatically.

Streaming AnalyticsDynamic BaselinesZone Segmentation
03

Predictive Risk Scoring

Anomalies are combined with historical patterns to generate a 0-100 risk score for each zone. Scores refresh on a rolling basis — your risk posture is always current, not a snapshot from yesterday.

ML Risk ModelsRolling ScoresPer-Zone Granularity

Explainable Threshold Alerts

When a zone's score crosses your defined threshold, RiskSignal sends a plain-language alert with full explainability — the top contributing factors, trend data, and recommended actions — to the right stakeholders immediately.
