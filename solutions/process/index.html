3-Phase Delivery Model

From Diagnostic to Scale.

A clear, low-risk path to enterprise-wide AI transformation with measurable business outcomes. We don't just hand over a tool; we integrate a capability.

01
Phase 1 • 4–6 Weeks

AI Readiness Diagnostic

Mapping the potential against reality.

We begin by auditing your structural data maturity and business workflows. Through intensive executive workshops, we isolate specific pain points where automation or cognitive insight generates undeniable ROI. We analyze your systems, pinpoint legacy debt, and output a strict architectural blueprint.

Executive Workshops Data Maturity Assessment Gap Analysis ROI Projections
02
Phase 2 • 6–8 Weeks

ROI-Driven Proof of Concept

Proving value before massive scale.

Risk mitigation is paramount. We select the highest-value use case identified in Phase 1 and build a focused, sandbox-deployed generative agent or automation workflow. Real enterprise data is securely used to validate exact KPI changes—be it processing time reduction or error rate drops.

Secure Sandbox Deployment Shadow Testing KPI Measurement Track Stakeholder Review
03
Phase 3 • Ongoing

Integration & Scale

Weaving intelligence into operations.

Following a successful POC gate, we move to full production deployment. This phase includes bridging APIs to live ERP/CRM systems, establishing robust human-in-the-loop (HITL) guardrails, and shifting focus to change management so your workforce correctly adopts the new AI collaboration paradigm.

Production Migration API Integration Ongoing Monitoring Change Management

Bridging AI's promise and your business reality
